Flower Arrangement

There are different types of arrangemnts and designs for flowers. Below are just a few types with examples.

S-SHAPED

The flowers are arranged to form the shape of an ’S’ and show beautiful and elegant curved lines. Leaves are typically used as a filler for flowers to appear more prominent.

This arrangement is also knows as ‘Hogarth Design’, and typically used for special occasions.

Flower Alternatives

There are alternatives to using real flowers. Below are a few examples to make flowers and their different uses.

WATERCOLOR

Painted flowers are visually beautiful and watercolor is a popular choice. Like any other watercolor project, flowers can be used. Examples would include postcards, bookmarks, interior display, or gifts.
